# Lesson 05: the staircase

**Start here:** look at the preview panel on the right.

You know `const`: a name that never changes. This lesson is `let`, a name that
can, and a staircase that makes you need one.

1. **Watch the counter** climb. Press `height += 1` and see one value take a
   new value each press. Then meet the room: three bars, each taller than the
   last, and a first-bar height the room picks, not you. Run a move with the
   heights typed in and watch it fit one room and miss the other. Then ask
   `robot.startHeight();` in each.
2. **Build the staircase**, by finishing `paintStaircase` in `src/exercise.ts`
   (open on the left). The first bar is done. Your job is the two taller ones:
   give the height a name you can change, and grow it by one for each. Save,
   then press the button as often as you like.

When the verdict turns green and reads `PASS`, both rooms stand. You do not
need the terminal for this lesson. The preview is your test.

## After the staircase

The preview shows the card: the words for what you just wrote. Read it, tell
your teacher what `let` gives you that `const` did not, then open lesson 6.

## Rules

- Edit `src/exercise.ts` only. `src/harness/` runs the world; leave it closed.
- `paintBar` and `goToNextBar` are done for you. Spend them; do not open them.
- `wordbook.md` lists every word so far. Look there before asking.
