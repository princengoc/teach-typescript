# Hints

**Task 1.** `>` means more than, and nothing else. Four is not more than four.

**Task 2.** `===` asks whether two values are the same one. Both sides here
are the number 3.

**Task 3.** `isShort` does not do the work itself; it calls `isLong` and puts
a `!` in front. Work out `isLong(9)` first, then flip it.
