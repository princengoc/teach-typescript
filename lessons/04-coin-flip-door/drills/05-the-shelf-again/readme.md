# 05: the shelf again

No booleans in this one. Lesson 03's words only: a function, its arguments,
and what it hands back.

1. `squaresIn(shelves)` hands back how many squares that many shelves hold.
   Every shelf is 3 squares long.
2. `pressesFor(shelves)` hands back how many calls it takes to paint them.
   One shelf takes 5 calls, and getting from one shelf to the next takes 4
   more.
