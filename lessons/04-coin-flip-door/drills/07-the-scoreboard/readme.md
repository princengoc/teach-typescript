# 07: the scoreboard

Spare drill. Two scores at the end of a game.

1. `isWin(mine, theirs)` says whether my score is the bigger one.
2. `isDraw(mine, theirs)` says whether the two scores are the same.
3. `result(mine, theirs)` hands back `'win'`, `'draw'` or `'lose'`. Three
   answers, so one `if` and one `else` are not enough: an `else` can hold
   another `if`.
