Lesson 2: paint the L

Last lesson you gave the robot a place to stand. This lesson you tell it what to do. You do that with a call: the robot's name, a dot, the thing you want, and a pair of brackets. 

robot.paint();

Press the buttons below and watch. Every press is one call, and the calls pile up into a list, top to bottom. That list is a program.

The robot only paints the square it is standing on, so step and paint take turns. Walk into a wall and it crashes.

Paint the L

Write your calls in src/exercise.ts (open on the left) and save. The dashed squares are the L you are aiming for.

  1. Finish the tall arm: one more step, one more paint.
  2. Turn the corner: robot.turnLeft();
  3. Paint the foot: two more squares, stepping onto each one first.

Well done! You wrote your first program.
