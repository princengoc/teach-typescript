# Lesson 02: paint the L

**Start here:** look at the preview panel on the right.

1. **Try the calls** first. Press the buttons and watch the robot. Every press
   is one call, and your presses pile up into a list. That list is a program.
2. **Paint the L**, by writing calls in `src/exercise.ts` (open on the left).
   The dashed squares are the shape you are aiming for. Save after each change
   and watch the preview replay your calls.

When the verdict turns green and reads `PASS`, you are done. You do not need
the terminal for this lesson. The preview is your test.

## After the L is painted

The preview shows you two things: the same calls you wrote painting the L from
a different door, and then the card with the words for what you did. Read
them, tell your teacher what each of your calls does, then open lesson 3.

## Rules

- Edit `src/exercise.ts` only. `src/harness/` runs the world; leave it closed.
- `card.md` is yours to keep. The preview renders it, so you never have to open
  it, but it is there when you want to look something up.
- `wordbook.md` lists every word so far. Look there before asking.
